Pizza Recipe

This is the piece you are making today!
This is the pizza recipe from my home of Italy. My family has spent years perfecting this recipe and know I hand it to you. You better take good care of this or you will ruin the reputation of my family. We will have you all killed if you do not make this recipe correctly.

Start by preheating your oven up to 350 degrees F. And check to make sure you have everything in the ingredients list.

Ingredients
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Goat's Blood
  • Tomato Sauce
  • The Blood of Your Enemy
  • All-Purpose Flour
Steps
  1. Start by mixing the bloods and tomato sauce. You want to make sure there is no lumps or hair in it.
  2. Next, get a circular shaped pan and pour the flour to the top, but no higher and no lower.
  3. Then, put the blood-tomato sauce mixture on to the pan of flour ensuring there is an even spread over the whole top, but none must fall off.
  4. Finally, throw that mother into the oven and let it stay in there for 33 and a half minutes on the dot.
